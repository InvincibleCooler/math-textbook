# for

> '~을 향해/위해' — 목적, 대상, 기간, 대가

## 감각 확장
### 목적·위함
- I study for the test.
- This gift is for you.

### 기간
- I've lived here for 3 years.
- Wait for a moment.

### 대가·교환
- I paid $10 for this.
- Thank you for your help.

### 찬성·지지
- I voted for her.
- Are you for or against the plan?

### 이유
- Seoul is famous for its food.
- He was fined for speeding.

## 동사 조합
| 패턴 | 예문 |
|go for|I'll go for the steak.|
|look for|I'm looking for my keys.|
|wait for|Wait for me!|
|ask for|She asked for help.|
|make up for|He made up for his mistake.|
|stand for|What does NASA stand for?|

## vs during (기간)
- **for**: 기간의 길이 — for 3 hours
- **during**: 특정 이벤트 동안 — during the meeting

## 연습
1. I've been waiting _____ an hour. (for / since)
2. What is this tool _____? (for / to)
3. She's looking _____ a new apartment. (for / at)

**정답**: 1. for  2. for  3. for
