# since

> '시작점부터 지금까지' — 기원과 이유

## 감각
### 시간: ~이래로
- I've lived here since 2020.
- She's been working since morning.

### 이유: ~이므로
- Since you asked, I'll tell you.
- Since it's raining, let's stay home.

## vs for
- **since**: 시작 시점 — since Monday (월요일부터)
- **for**: 기간 길이 — for 3 days (3일 동안)

## 함정
- (X) I live here since 2020. → (O) I've lived here since 2020. (현재완료 필수)

## 연습
1. She's been sick _____ last week. (since / for)
2. _____ you're here, can you help me? (Since / For)

**정답**: 1. since  2. Since
