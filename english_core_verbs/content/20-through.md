# through

> 처음부터 끝까지 '뚫고 지나간다' — 관통, 경험, 완수

## 감각 확장: 공간 → 경험 → 완수

### 공간: 관통
한쪽에서 반대쪽으로 뚫고 지나가는 감각.
- We drove through the tunnel.
- Light shines through the window.

### 경험: 겪다
어려움을 처음부터 끝까지 뚫고 지나가는 감각.
- She went through a difficult divorce.
- I've been through a lot this year.

### 완수: 끝까지
시작점에서 끝점까지 관통하여 마무리하는 감각.
- Read the book all the way through.
- Follow through on your promises.

### 매개: 수단
무언가를 "통해서" 도달하는 감각.
- I found the job through a friend.
- You can apply through the website.

## 동사 조합 TOP
| 동사 + through | 감각 | 예문 |
|----------------|------|------|
| go through | 겪다 / 통과하다 | He's going through a tough time. |
| get through | 끝내다 / 연결되다 | I can't get through to her phone. |
| come through | 해내다 / 도착하다 | He always comes through in a crisis. |
| break through | 돌파하다 | They broke through the enemy lines. |
| see through | 꿰뚫어 보다 | I can see through his lies. |
| put through | 연결하다 / 겪게 하다 | I'll put you through to the manager. |

## vs across
- **through**: 안을 뚫고 지나감 — through the forest (숲 안을 관통)
- **across**: 표면을 가로지름 — across the field (들판을 가로질러)

## 함정
- (X) I went through from a hard time. → (O) I went through a hard time.
- (X) Read through of the document. → (O) Read through the document.
