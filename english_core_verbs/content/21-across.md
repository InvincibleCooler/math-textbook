# across

> 평면을 '가로질러' 반대편으로

## 감각 확장
### 공간: 가로질러
- She walked across the street.
- The bridge goes across the river.

### 우연히 만남: 가로지르다가 마주침
- I came across an old friend at the store.
- She ran across an interesting article.

### 전달·이해: 반대편에 도달
- Did the message get across?
- She got her point across clearly.

## 동사 조합 TOP
| 동사 + across | 감각 | 예문 |
|come across|우연히 발견|I came across this book by accident.|
|get across|전달하다|How do I get my idea across?|
|run across|우연히 만나다|I ran across her at the mall.|
|cut across|가로지르다|Let's cut across the park.|

## 함정
- (X) I came across with a problem. → (O) I came across a problem.

## 연습
1. She _____ across an old photo in the drawer. (came / went)
2. Did the meaning get _____ to the audience? (across / through)
3. They walked _____ the bridge at sunset. (across / through)

**정답**: 1. came  2. across  3. across
