# at

> 정확한 한 '점'을 찍는다 — 지점, 시각, 겨냥

## 감각 확장
### 지점: 한 점
넓은 면이 아닌 점 하나를 가리키는 감각.
- I'm at the station.
- She's at home. / He's at work.

### 시각: 정확한 시점
- The meeting starts at 3 o'clock.
- I wake up at 6 every day.

### 겨냥: 향해 쏘다
시선이나 행동이 한 점을 향하는 감각.
- Look at me.
- She laughed at his joke.
- Don't shout at me!

### 능력·상태
어떤 점에 위치해 있는 감각.
- She's good at math.
- He's bad at cooking.

### 속도·가격
- driving at 60mph
- selling at $5 each

## vs in
- **at**: 점 (좁은 지점) — at the bus stop
- **in**: 면·공간 (넓은 곳) — in the city

## 함정
- (X) I'm good in math. → (O) I'm good at math.
- (X) Look at to me. → (O) Look at me.
- (X) She arrived in the airport. → (O) She arrived at the airport.

## 연습
1. The train arrives _____ 5:30. (at / in)
2. She's really good _____ singing. (at / in)
3. He threw the ball _____ me. (at / to) — at은 겨냥(공격적), to는 전달

**정답**: 1. at  2. at  3. at (던져 맞추다) / to (건네주다)
