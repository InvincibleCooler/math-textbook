# than

> 비교의 '기준점'

## 감각
### 비교
- She's taller than me.
- This is more expensive than I thought.
- I'd rather stay home than go out.

### other than: ~외에
- I have no choice other than to accept.

### more than / less than
- More than 100 people came.
- It costs less than $10.

## 함정
- (X) She's taller then me. → (O) She's taller than me. (then은 "그 다음에")
- (X) I prefer coffee than tea. → (O) I prefer coffee to tea. (prefer는 to)

## 연습
1. He runs faster _____ anyone else. (than / then)
2. I'd rather walk _____ drive. (than / to)

**정답**: 1. than  2. than
