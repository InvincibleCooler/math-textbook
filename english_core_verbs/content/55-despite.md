# despite (in spite of)

> '~에도 불구하고' — 양보

## 감각
예상과 반대되는 결과. 장애물이 있지만 결과는 달랐다.
- Despite the rain, we had a great time.
- She passed the exam despite being sick.
- In spite of the traffic, we arrived on time.

## despite vs although
- **despite** + 명사/-ing: Despite the cold, ...
- **although** + 절: Although it was cold, ...

## 함정
- (X) Despite of the rain → (O) Despite the rain (of 불필요)
- (X) Despite it rained → (O) Despite the rain / Although it rained

## 연습
1. _____ his age, he runs every morning. (Despite / Although)
2. She succeeded _____ all the difficulties. (despite / although)

**정답**: 1. Despite  2. despite
