# around

> 둥글게 '주변을' — 주위, 여기저기, 대략, 존재

## 감각 확장
### 공간: 주위에 / 여기저기
- Look around. It's beautiful here.
- She traveled around Europe.

### 대략
- There were around 50 people.
- I'll be there around 3.

### 존재: 근처에 있음
- Is Tom around?
- He's been around for a long time.

### 회피: 둘러감
- We need to get around this problem.
- There's no way around it.

## 동사 조합 TOP
| 동사 + around | 감각 | 예문 |
|get around|돌아다니다 / 회피|How do you get around the city?|
|come around|들르다 / 마음 바꾸다|She'll come around eventually.|
|turn around|돌아서다 / 호전|The company turned around in a year.|
|hang around|어슬렁거리다|Stop hanging around and do something.|

## 함정
- (X) around about 50 → (O) around 50 (중복)

## 연습
1. There were _____ 200 guests at the wedding. (around / about) — 둘 다 가능
2. Is your manager _____? (around / here) — 둘 다 가능
3. We need to find a way _____ this issue. (around / through)

**정답**: 1. around/about  2. around/here  3. around
