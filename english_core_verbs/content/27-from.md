# from

> '출발점' — 어디서부터인지, to의 반대

## 감각 확장
### 출발점
- I'm from Korea.
- The train departs from platform 3.

### 원인·근거
- She suffers from allergies.
- I know from experience.

### 분리·차이
- This is different from that.
- Keep him away from trouble.

### 재료·출처
- Wine is made from grapes.
- A quote from the book.

## 동사 조합
| 패턴 | 예문 |
|come from|Where do you come from?|
|get from|I got this from a friend.|
|keep from|I can't keep from laughing.|
|prevent from|Rain prevented us from going out.|
|suffer from|He suffers from back pain.|

## vs of (재료)
- **from**: 원형을 알 수 없음 — Wine is made from grapes.
- **of**: 원형이 보임 — The table is made of wood.

## 연습
1. Where are you _____? (from / of)
2. She suffers _____ migraines. (from / with)
3. This dress is made _____ silk. (of / from) — silk이 보이니 of

**정답**: 1. from  2. from  3. of
