# of

> '~로부터 나온/~에 속한' — 영어에서 가장 빈도 높은 전치사

## 감각
### 소속·부분
- A cup of coffee.
- The roof of the building.
- One of my friends.

### 관련·대상
- I'm afraid of spiders.
- She's proud of her son.
- Think of a number.

### 분리·박탈
- He robbed me of my wallet.
- The city was emptied of people.

### 원인
- He died of cancer.

### 특성
- A man of courage.
- A woman of great talent.

### 재료 (원형 보임)
- A table made of wood.

## 함정
- (X) a cup of a coffee → (O) a cup of coffee
- (X) I'm afraid from spiders. → (O) I'm afraid of spiders.
- (X) He died from cancer. → (O) He died of cancer. (질병 원인은 of)

## 연습
1. She's one _____ the best singers. (of / in)
2. A glass _____ water, please. (of / with)
3. I'm thinking _____ you. (of / about) — 둘 다 가능

**정답**: 1. of  2. of  3. of/about
