# off

> 표면에서 '떨어져 나간다' — on의 반대, 분리와 중단

## 감각 확장: 분리 → 중단 → 완료 → 출발

### 분리: 떨어짐
붙어있던 것이 떨어져 나가는 감각.
- The button fell off my shirt.
- Take off your shoes at the door.

### 중단: 끄기
작동이 "떨어지는" → 멈추는 감각.
- Turn off the TV.
- The meeting was called off.

### 완료: 해치움
떨어뜨려서 끝내는 감각.
- Pay off your debt.
- She finished off the last piece of cake.

### 출발: 떠남
"여기서 떨어져 나가는" → 출발.
- We're off to Paris tomorrow.
- The plane takes off at 9.

### 할인: 깎아냄
가격에서 떨어뜨리는 감각.
- 20% off everything today.
- Can you take $10 off?

## 동사 조합 TOP
| 동사 + off | 감각 | 예문 |
|------------|------|------|
| take off | 벗다 / 이륙 / 떠나다 | The plane took off on time. |
| turn off | 끄다 | Turn off the lights. |
| put off | 미루다 | Don't put off your work. |
| go off | 울리다 / 폭발 | The alarm went off. |
| get off | 내리다 | Get off at the next stop. |
| hold off | 보류하다 | Let's hold off for now. |
| give off | 발산하다 | Roses give off a sweet smell. |

## vs out
- **off**: 표면에서 떨어짐 — turn off (끄다, 스위치에서 떨어짐)
- **out**: 안에서 밖으로 — go out (밖으로 나가다)

## 함정
- (X) I get off from the bus. → (O) I get off the bus. (from 불필요)
- (X) The meeting called off. → (O) The meeting was called off. (수동태)
- (X) Turn off it. → (O) Turn it off. (대명사는 사이에)

## 연습
1. Please _____ off your phone during the movie. (turn / put)
2. The plane _____ off 30 minutes late. (took / got)
3. She _____ off the meeting until next week. (put / held)
4. Everything is 30% _____ today. (off / down)
5. The fire alarm _____ off at midnight. (went / turned)

**정답**: 1. turn  2. took  3. put  4. off  5. went
