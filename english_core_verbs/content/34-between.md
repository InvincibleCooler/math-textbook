# between

> 두 지점 '사이에'

## 감각
### 공간·시간: 두 개 사이
- The bank is between the cafe and the bookstore.
- Between 9 and 5.

### 선택
- Choose between A and B.
- It's hard to decide between the two.

### 관계: 둘 사이의 일
- This stays between us.
- There's a big difference between them.

## vs among
- **between**: 둘 사이 — between you and me
- **among**: 셋 이상 무리 속 — among friends

## 연습
1. The secret is _____ you and me. (between / among)
2. She couldn't choose _____ the two options. (between / from)

**정답**: 1. between  2. between
