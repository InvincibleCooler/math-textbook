# out

> 경계 '밖으로' 나온다

## 감각 확장: 공간 → 출현 → 소멸 → 완료

### 공간: 밖으로
안에서 바깥으로 나오는 감각.
- Get out of the house!
- She walked out of the room.

### 출현·드러남
숨어있던 것이 밖으로 나와 보이는 감각.
- The truth came out.
- It turns out he was lying.

### 소멸·소진
다 나가서 없어지는 감각.
- We ran out of milk.
- The fire went out.

### 완료·끝까지
안에 있던 걸 전부 꺼내는 → 끝까지 하는 감각.
- Figure out the answer. (끝까지 알아내다)
- Work out a solution. (끝까지 해결하다)

## 동사 조합 TOP
| 동사 + out | 감각 | 예문 |
|------------|------|------|
| get out | 나가다 | Get out of here! |
| find out | 알아내다 | I need to find out the truth. |
| turn out | 결과가 ~이 되다 | It turned out fine. |
| go out | 나가다 / 꺼지다 | The lights went out. |
| take out | 꺼내다 | Take out your wallet. |
| make out | 알아보다 | I can't make out the sign. |
| hold out | 버티다 | Hold out a little longer. |

## vs off
- **out**: 안에서 밖으로 나옴 — go out (밖으로 나가다)
- **off**: 표면에서 떨어져 나옴 — go off (울리다/폭발하다)

## 함정
- (X) I ran out milk. → (O) I ran out of milk. (of 필요)
- (X) Find out it. → (O) Find it out. 또는 Find out the answer. (대명사는 사이에)
- (X) The secret came out to everyone. → (O) The secret came out. (to 불필요)

## 연습
1. We _____ out of time. Hurry up! (ran / went)
2. She finally _____ out what happened. (found / got)
3. It _____ out to be easier than I thought. (turned / came)
4. _____ out your homework and put it on the desk. (Take / Put)
5. The candle _____ out in the wind. (went / turned)

**정답**: 1. ran  2. found  3. turned  4. Take  5. went
