# during

> 어떤 이벤트가 '진행되는 동안'

## 감각
- I fell asleep during the movie.
- Don't use your phone during class.

## vs for
- **during**: 이벤트 동안 — during the meeting (회의라는 이벤트)
- **for**: 기간의 길이 — for 2 hours (2시간이라는 길이)

## 함정
- (X) during 3 hours → (O) for 3 hours (숫자 기간은 for)
- (X) during I was sleeping → (O) while I was sleeping (절이면 while)

## 연습
1. It rained _____ the night. (during / for)
2. I studied _____ 3 hours. (for / during)

**정답**: 1. during  2. for
