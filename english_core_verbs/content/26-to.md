# to

> '향하는 지점' — 영어에서 가장 기본적인 방향 감각

## 감각 확장
### 방향·도착점
- I'm going to the office.
- She talked to her boss.

### 결과·변화
- It led to a disaster.
- She grew up to be a doctor.

### 비교
- I prefer coffee to tea.
- Compared to last year, sales are up.

### 시간 한계
- It's 10 to 3. (3시 10분 전)
- Monday to Friday.

## 동사 조합
| 패턴 | 예문 |
|get to|I finally got to meet her.|
|come to|The bill comes to $80.|
|turn to|She turned to me for help.|
|belong to|This book belongs to me.|
|lead to|Hard work leads to success.|

## vs for
- **to**: 도착점 — go to the store
- **for**: 목적·위함 — go for a walk

## 연습
1. She went _____ the hospital. (to / for)
2. This leads _____ only one conclusion. (to / for)
3. I prefer tea _____ coffee. (to / than)

**정답**: 1. to  2. to  3. to
