# beside / besides

> beside: '옆에' / besides: '게다가'

## 감각
### beside: 옆에 (위치)
- She sat beside me.
- The park is right beside the school.

### besides: 게다가 / ~외에 (추가)
- Besides English, she speaks French.
- I'm tired. Besides, it's too late.

## 함정
- beside와 besides를 혼동하지 말 것
- beside = next to (위치)
- besides = in addition to (추가)

## 연습
1. Who was sitting _____ you? (beside / besides)
2. _____ the cost, there are other problems. (Besides / Beside)

**정답**: 1. beside  2. Besides
