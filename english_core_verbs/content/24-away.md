# away

> 여기서 '멀어진다' — 거리, 사라짐, 몰두

## 감각 확장
### 거리: 멀어짐
- Go away! Leave me alone.
- The store is 5 minutes away.

### 사라짐: 없어짐
- The pain went away.
- She gave away all her old clothes.

### 몰두: 계속 (멀리 가듯 빠져듦)
- She was typing away all afternoon.
- They chatted away for hours.

### 보관: 치워둠
- Put away your toys.
- She locked away the jewelry.

## 동사 조합 TOP
| 동사 + away | 감각 | 예문 |
|go away|떠나다 / 사라지다|The headache won't go away.|
|give away|나눠주다 / 누설|Don't give away the ending.|
|take away|가져가다 / 빼앗다|Nothing can take away this memory.|
|put away|치우다|Put away your phone during dinner.|
|run away|도망치다|The dog ran away from home.|
|get away|탈출하다|I need to get away for a few days.|

## 함정
- (X) Go to away. → (O) Go away. (to 불필요)
- (X) She threw away it. → (O) She threw it away.

## 연습
1. The noise won't go _____. (away / off)
2. She _____ away her old books. (gave / put)
3. We need to get _____ from the city. (away / out)

**정답**: 1. away  2. gave  3. away
