# among

> 셋 이상의 무리 '속에'

## 감각
### 무리 속에
- She sat among her friends.
- He's popular among students.

### 포함
- Among other things, we discussed the budget.
- She's among the best in the field.

## vs between
- **among**: 무리 속 — among the crowd
- **between**: 둘 사이 — between two people

## 연습
1. He distributed the candy _____ the children. (among / between)
2. _____ other issues, we talked about salary. (Among / Between)

**정답**: 1. among  2. Among
