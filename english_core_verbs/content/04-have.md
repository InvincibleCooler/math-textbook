# have

> 내 영역에 '가지고 있다' — 소유와 경험의 동사

## 감각 지도

### 1. 소유·보유 ★★★★★
내 영역 안에 존재하는 감각.
- I have two brothers.
- Do you have any questions?

### 2. 먹다·마시다 ★★★★★
음식을 내 영역(몸)으로 가져오는 감각. eat/drink보다 부드러운 표현.
- Let's have lunch together.
- I'll have a coffee, please.

### 3. 경험하다 ★★★★
어떤 시간·상황을 겪어서 가지는 감각.
- We had a great time at the party.
- I had trouble finding the place.

### 4. 시키다 (have + 목적어 + 동사) ★★★
서비스나 행위를 가지게 되는 감각.
- I had my hair cut yesterday.
- I'll have someone call you back.

### 5. 해야 한다 (have to) ★★★★★
의무를 가지고 있는 감각.
- I have to go now.
- You don't have to worry about it.

## 필수 패턴
| 패턴 | 감각 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| have to + 동사 | 의무 | I have to finish this today. |
| have + 목적어 + p.p. | 시키다 (완료) | I had my car fixed. |
| have + 목적어 + 동사원형 | 시키다 (진행) | I'll have him check it. |
| have been to | 가본 적 있다 | Have you been to Paris? |
| have something to do with | 관련 있다 | It has nothing to do with me. |
| have no idea | 전혀 모르다 | I have no idea what happened. |

## 함정
- (X) I have 25 years old. → (O) I'm 25 years old. (나이는 be 동사)
- (X) I have a headache since morning. → (O) I've had a headache since morning. (since = 현재완료)
- (X) I had cut my hair. → (O) I had my hair cut. (시키다 구문 어순)

## 연습

1. Let's _____ dinner at that new restaurant. (have / eat)
2. I _____ to leave early tomorrow. (have / must)
3. She _____ her nails done at the salon. (had / made)
4. We _____ a wonderful time on vacation. (had / got)
5. This _____ nothing to do with you. (has / is)

**정답**: 1. have  2. have  3. had  4. had  5. has
