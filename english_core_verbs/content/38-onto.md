# onto

> 표면 '위로 올라가는 동작' — on이 결과면 onto는 과정

## 감각
### 위로 올라감
- The cat jumped onto the table.
- She climbed onto the roof.

### 붙잡음
- Hold onto the railing.
- She held onto hope.

## vs on
- **onto**: 이동 (동적) — jump onto the stage
- **on**: 위치 (정적) — stand on the stage

## 연습
1. The dog jumped _____ the couch. (onto / on)
2. Hold _____ the rope tightly. (onto / on)

**정답**: 1. onto  2. onto
