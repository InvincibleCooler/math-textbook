# in

> 어떤 경계 '안에' 들어가 있다

## 감각 확장: 공간 → 시간 → 추상

### 공간: 경계 안
닫힌 공간 속에 존재하는 감각.
- The keys are in my pocket.
- She's in the kitchen.

### 시간: 범위 안
넓은 시간 덩어리 속에 있는 감각. 월, 연도, 기간.
- I was born in 1990.
- I'll be back in 10 minutes. (10분 후 = 10분이라는 시간 안에)

### 추상: 상태·수단 안
보이지 않는 경계 안에 있는 감각.
- She's in trouble. / He's in love.
- Say it in English. / Pay in cash.

## 동사 조합 TOP
| 동사 + in | 감각 | 예문 |
|-----------|------|------|
| get in | 안으로 들어가다 | Get in the car. |
| come in | 들어오다 | Come in and sit down. |
| put in | 넣다 / 투입하다 | She put in a lot of hours. |
| give in | 굴복하다 | He finally gave in. |
| turn in | 제출하다 / 잠자리 들다 | Turn in your assignment by Friday. |
| bring in | 도입하다 | They brought in a new policy. |

## vs at
- **in**: 경계 안 (넓은 공간) — in Seoul, in the room
- **at**: 한 점 (좁은 지점) — at the station, at home

## 함정
- (X) in the morning / (X) on the morning → (O) in the morning (아침이라는 시간 덩어리 안)
- (X) in Monday → (O) on Monday (요일은 on)
- (X) arrive in the airport → (O) arrive at the airport (지점은 at)

## 연습
1. She lives _____ a small town. (in / at)
2. I'll see you _____ 30 minutes. (in / after)
3. He's _____ trouble with the boss. (in / on)
4. Get _____ the car. We're leaving. (in / on)
5. The meeting is _____ March. (in / on)

**정답**: 1. in  2. in  3. in  4. in  5. in
