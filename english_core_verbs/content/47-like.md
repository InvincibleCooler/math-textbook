# like

> '닮은' — ~처럼, ~같은

## 감각
### ~처럼
- She looks like her mother.
- It feels like summer.
- What's it like living abroad?

### ~같은
- Something like that.
- I want a car like yours.

### ~하고 싶은 (feel like)
- I feel like eating pizza.
- I don't feel like going out.

## vs as
- **like**: 닮음 (뒤에 명사) — She sings like a pro.
- **as**: 자격 (뒤에 절 가능) — She sings as a pro does.

## 함정
- (X) Like I said... → 구어에서 많이 쓰지만, 문법적으로 As I said...가 정확

## 연습
1. What does it taste _____? (like / as)
2. I feel _____ staying home today. (like / as)

**정답**: 1. like  2. like
