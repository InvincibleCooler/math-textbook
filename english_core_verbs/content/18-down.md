# down

> '아래로' 내려간다 — 감소, 안정, 기록의 감각

## 감각 확장: 아래로 → 감소 → 기록 → 안정 → 중단 → 거절

### 아래로: 물리적 하강
위에서 아래로 내려오는 감각.
- Sit down, please.
- Put it down on the table.

### 감소: 줄어듦
수치나 강도가 내려가는 감각.
- Slow down. You're driving too fast.
- Calm down. It's going to be okay.
- Turn down the volume.

### 기록: 적어 내림
머릿속에서 종이 위로 "내려놓는" 감각.
- Write down your phone number.
- Let me note that down.

### 안정·고정: 자리 잡음
아래로 내려와서 움직이지 않는 감각.
- Settle down and focus.
- Pin down the details.

### 중단·폐쇄: 멈춤
아래로 내려서 작동을 멈추는 감각.
- The system shut down.
- The factory broke down.

### 거절: 내려놓음
제안을 아래로 내리는 감각.
- She turned down the offer.
- He let me down. (실망시키다)

## 동사 조합 TOP
| 동사 + down | 감각 | 예문 |
|-------------|------|------|
| sit down | 앉다 | Please sit down. |
| turn down | 거절 / 볼륨 낮추기 | She turned down the job. |
| break down | 고장 / 무너지다 | My car broke down. |
| calm down | 진정하다 | Calm down and listen. |
| put down | 내려놓다 / 비하 | Put down your phone. |
| write down | 적다 | Write down the address. |
| come down to | 결국 ~에 달려 있다 | It comes down to money. |

## vs up
- **down**: 감소·안정·기록 — calm down, write down
- **up**: 증가·완료·출현 — speed up, eat up

## 함정
- (X) Turn down it. → (O) Turn it down. (대명사는 사이에)
- (X) She broke down her car. → (O) Her car broke down. (자동사)
- (X) Write down it. → (O) Write it down.

## 연습
1. Can you _____ down the music? (turn / put)
2. She _____ down and started to cry. (broke / turned)
3. _____ down your name and email. (Write / Put)
4. The kids need to _____ down before bed. (calm / slow)
5. He _____ me down when I needed him most. (let / put)

**정답**: 1. turn  2. broke  3. Write  4. calm  5. let
