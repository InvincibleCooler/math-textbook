# over

> 아치형으로 '넘어간다' — 끝까지 넘어가면 종료, 다시 돌아오면 반복

## 감각 확장: 공간 → 종료 → 반복 → 초과

### 공간: 위를 넘어
장애물 위를 아치형으로 넘어가는 감각.
- The cat jumped over the fence.
- She leaned over the balcony.

### 종료: 끝남
완전히 넘어가서 끝나는 감각.
- The game is over.
- I'm glad it's over.

### 반복: 다시 넘기
처음으로 돌아가서 다시 넘는 감각.
- Let's start over.
- Can you do it over?

### 초과·전체
경계를 넘는 → 넘치는 감각.
- She's over 40.
- I've been thinking it over. (이리저리 전체를)

## 동사 조합 TOP
| 동사 + over | 감각 | 예문 |
|-------------|------|------|
| get over | 넘어서 극복하다 | Get over it. It's not a big deal. |
| go over | 넘어가며 검토하다 | Let's go over the plan again. |
| take over | 넘겨받다 | She took over the project. |
| turn over | 뒤집다 | Turn over the page. |
| come over | 넘어와 방문하다 | Come over to my place. |
| hand over | 넘겨주다 | Hand over the documents. |

## vs above
- **over**: 위를 넘어가는 동적 감각 — jump over
- **above**: 위에 떠 있는 정적 감각 — above the clouds

## 함정
- (X) The game is over already? → (O) 맞음. 자연스러운 표현.
- (X) I need to get over from my cold. → (O) I need to get over my cold. (from 불필요)

## 연습
1. The meeting is _____. You can leave. (over / out)
2. Let's go _____ the details one more time. (over / through)
3. She got _____ her fear of heights. (over / out)
4. Can we start _____? I made a mistake. (over / up)
5. He _____ over the company last year. (took / got)

**정답**: 1. over  2. over  3. over  4. over  5. took
