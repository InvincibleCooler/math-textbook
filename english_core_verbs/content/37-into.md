# into

> 밖에서 '안으로 들어가는 동작' — in이 결과면 into는 과정

## 감각
### 안으로 (동작)
- She walked into the room.
- Pour the water into the glass.

### 변화: ~으로 바뀜
- The frog turned into a prince.
- They broke into tears.

### 충돌: 들이받음
- He ran into a wall.
- I bumped into an old friend. (우연히 만남)

### 몰두
- She got into music. (빠지다)
- He's really into cooking these days.

## vs in
- **into**: 이동·변화 (동적) — walk into the room
- **in**: 위치·상태 (정적) — be in the room

## 연습
1. She poured coffee _____ the cup. (into / in)
2. He's really _____ photography these days. (into / in)
3. The caterpillar turned _____ a butterfly. (into / to)

**정답**: 1. into  2. into  3. into
