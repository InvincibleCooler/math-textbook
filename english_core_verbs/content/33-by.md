# by

> 바로 '옆을 지나' — 근접, 수단, 기한, 차이

## 감각 확장
### 옆에: 근접
- She sat by the window.
- The house by the lake.

### 수단: ~에 의해 / ~로
- Made by Apple. / Travel by train.
- Pay by credit card.

### 기한: ~까지
- Finish it by Friday.
- I'll be there by 5.

### 차이
- She won by 2 points.
- He missed the train by 5 minutes.

### 단위
- one by one / day by day / step by step

## 동사 조합
| 패턴 | 예문 |
|go by|Time goes by so fast.|
|get by|We can get by on this budget.|
|stand by|I'll stand by you no matter what.|
|come by|Come by my office later.|
|pass by|She passed by without noticing me.|

## vs until (기한)
- **by**: ~까지 (그 전에 완료) — Finish by Friday.
- **until**: ~까지 (그때까지 계속) — I'll wait until Friday.

## 연습
1. The report must be done _____ Monday. (by / until)
2. She goes to work _____ bus. (by / with)
3. He won the race _____ a second. (by / with)

**정답**: 1. by  2. by  3. by
