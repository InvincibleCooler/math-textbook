# beyond

> 저 '너머로' — over보다 더 먼 감각

## 감각
### 공간: 넘어서
- The village is just beyond the hill.

### 초과: 범위 밖
- It's beyond my understanding.
- This is beyond repair.
- She's beautiful beyond words.

## 연습
1. The answer is _____ my knowledge. (beyond / over)
2. The park is just _____ the bridge. (beyond / across)

**정답**: 1. beyond  2. beyond
