# against

> '맞서다/맞닿다' — 반대, 접촉, 대비, 방어

## 감각
### 반대
- I'm against the idea.
- It's against the rules.

### 맞닿음·기댐
- She leaned against the wall.
- The rain hit against the window.

### 대비·방어
- Save money against a rainy day.
- Vaccinate against the flu.

### 배경
- The building stood out against the blue sky.

## 연습
1. Are you for or _____ the proposal? (against / with)
2. She leaned _____ the door. (against / on)
3. We need protection _____ hackers. (against / from) — 둘 다 가능

**정답**: 1. against  2. against  3. against/from
