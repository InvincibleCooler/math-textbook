# after

> '뒤를 따라' — 시간 후, 추적, ~을 따서

## 감각
### 시간: ~후에
- I'll call you after lunch.
- After the movie, we went home.

### 추적: 뒤를 쫓다
- The police went after the thief.
- She takes after her mother. (닮다)

### 돌봄: 뒤를 챙기다
- Who looks after the kids?

### ~을 따서
- She was named after her grandmother.

## 동사 조합
| 패턴 | 예문 |
|look after|Look after yourself.|
|go after|Go after your dreams.|
|take after|He takes after his father.|

## 연습
1. I'll meet you _____ work. (after / later)
2. She _____ after her mother in looks. (takes / goes)
3. Who looks _____ the dog when you travel? (after / for)

**정답**: 1. after  2. takes  3. after
