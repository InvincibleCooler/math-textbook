# with

> '나란히 함께' — 동반, 도구, 원인, 특성

## 감각 확장
### 동반
- I went with my friend.
- I agree with you.

### 도구·수단
- Cut it with scissors.
- She opened it with a key.

### 원인·감정
- He was shaking with fear.
- She cried with joy.

### 특성·소유
- A man with glasses.
- A house with a garden.

### 관계
- What's wrong with it?
- I'm done with this.

## 동사 조합
| 패턴 | 예문 |
|deal with|How do you deal with stress?|
|go with|This shirt goes with those pants.|
|get along with|She gets along with everyone.|
|come up with|He came up with a great idea.|
|put up with|I can't put up with this anymore.|
|keep up with|I can't keep up with the news.|

## vs by (수단)
- **with**: 도구 (물리적) — write with a pen
- **by**: 방법·수단 — travel by train, pay by card

## 연습
1. She filled the cup _____ water. (with / of)
2. I can't deal _____ this anymore. (with / about)
3. He was trembling _____ cold. (with / from) — 둘 다 가능

**정답**: 1. with  2. with  3. with/from
