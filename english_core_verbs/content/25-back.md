# back

> 원래 자리로 '되돌아간다' — 복귀, 반환, 과거

## 감각 확장
### 복귀: 돌아옴
- I'll be back in 10 minutes.
- Welcome back!

### 반환: 돌려줌
- Give back my pen.
- I need to pay back the loan.

### 과거: 되돌아봄
- Looking back, it was a great decision.
- That takes me back to my childhood.

### 보복: 되갚음
- I'll get back at him for this.
- She fought back.

## 동사 조합 TOP
| 동사 + back | 감각 | 예문 |
|come back|돌아오다|When will you come back?|
|give back|돌려주다|Give it back to me.|
|get back|돌아가다 / 되찾다|I need to get back to work.|
|hold back|참다 / 억제하다|She held back her tears.|
|take back|취소하다 / 돌려받다|I take back what I said.|
|look back|되돌아보다|Don't look back. Move forward.|

## 함정
- (X) I'll call back to you. → (O) I'll call you back.
- (X) Give back it. → (O) Give it back.

## 연습
1. She'll be _____ by 5 o'clock. (back / around)
2. I want to get my money _____. (back / out)
3. He took _____ his words. (back / off)

**정답**: 1. back  2. back  3. back
