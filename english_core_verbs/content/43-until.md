# until (till)

> '그 시점에 도달할 때까지' 계속

## 감각
- I'll wait until 5 o'clock.
- Don't open it until Christmas.
- She didn't sleep until midnight.

## vs by
- **until**: ~까지 계속 — Wait until Friday. (금요일까지 계속 기다려)
- **by**: ~까지 완료 — Finish by Friday. (금요일 전에 끝내)

## 함정
- (X) I'll finish until Friday. → (O) I'll finish by Friday. (완료는 by)
- until = till (till이 더 캐주얼)

## 연습
1. The store is open _____ 10 p.m. (until / by)
2. I need to submit this _____ tomorrow. (by / until)

**정답**: 1. until  2. by
