# before

> '앞에' — 시간적으로 먼저, 공간적으로 앞에

## 감각
### 시간: ~전에
- I'll finish before noon.
- Think before you speak.

### 공간: 앞에 (격식)
- He appeared before the judge.

### 우선
- Health comes before money.

## vs ago
- **before**: 기준점 앞 — before lunch, before I left
- **ago**: 지금부터 거슬러 — 3 years ago

## 연습
1. I need to leave _____ 5 o'clock. (before / until)
2. She arrived _____ me. (before / ago)

**정답**: 1. before  2. before
