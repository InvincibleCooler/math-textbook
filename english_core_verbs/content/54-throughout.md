# throughout

> 처음부터 끝까지 '빠짐없이' — through + out

## 감각
### 기간 전체
- It rained throughout the day.
- She smiled throughout the interview.

### 공간 전체
- The disease spread throughout the country.
- Music could be heard throughout the building.

## vs during
- **throughout**: 처음부터 끝까지 전부 — throughout the year
- **during**: 그 기간 중 어느 시점 — during the year

## 연습
1. He traveled _____ Asia last summer. (throughout / through)
2. It snowed _____ the night. (throughout / during) — 둘 다 가능, throughout이 "밤새 내내"

**정답**: 1. throughout  2. throughout
