# about

> 주변을 '맴도는' 감각 — 주제, 대략, ~하려는 참

## 감각 확장
### 주제: ~에 관하여
핵심을 직접 찌르지 않고 그 주변을 도는 감각.
- What's the movie about?
- I'm worried about the exam.

### 대략
정확한 점이 아닌 그 근처.
- There were about 100 people.
- It's about 10 minutes away.

### ~하려는 참
행동의 바로 근처에 와 있는 감각.
- I'm about to leave.
- The train is about to depart.

## 동사 조합
| 패턴 | 예문 |
|talk about|Let's talk about the plan.|
|think about|I'll think about it.|
|worry about|Don't worry about it.|
|bring about|What brought about the change?|
|come about|How did this come about?|

## vs on (주제)
- **about**: 일반적 주제 — talk about music
- **on**: 학술적·공식적 — a lecture on economics

## 연습
1. What are you talking _____? (about / on)
2. She's _____ to start her presentation. (about / around)
3. There are _____ 50 students in the class. (about / around) — 둘 다 가능

**정답**: 1. about  2. about  3. about/around
