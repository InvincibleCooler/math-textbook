# make

> 없던 것을 '만들어내다' — 결과를 발생시키는 동사

## 감각 지도

### 1. 만들다 / 생산하다 ★★★★★
재료에서 결과물이 나오는 감각.
- She made dinner for the family.
- This car is made in Germany.

### 2. ~하게 만들다 (감정·상태) ★★★★★
누군가에게 변화를 일으키는 감각. 가장 많이 쓰는 용법.
- You make me happy.
- The noise made it hard to sleep.

### 3. 벌다 ★★★★
돈이라는 결과를 만들어내는 감각.
- He makes $5,000 a month.
- She made a fortune in real estate.

### 4. 결정·선택하다 ★★★
결정이라는 결과를 만드는 감각.
- I made a decision to quit.
- Let's make a plan for the weekend.

### 5. 도달하다 / 성공하다 ★★★
목표에 닿는 결과를 만드는 감각.
- We made it just in time.
- She finally made it to the top.

## 필수 패턴
| 패턴 | 감각 | 예문 |
|------|------|------|
| make + 목적어 + 형용사 | 상태 변화 | The rain made the road slippery. |
| make + 목적어 + 동사원형 | 시키다 | He made me wait for an hour. |
| make up | 구성 / 화해 / 꾸미다 | Let's make up. We shouldn't fight. |
| make out | 알아보다 / 이해하다 | I can't make out what he's saying. |
| make sure | 확인하다 | Make sure you lock the door. |
| make sense | 이치에 맞다 | That doesn't make sense. |
| make a difference | 변화를 만들다 | Every small action makes a difference. |

## 함정
- (X) She made me to cry. → (O) She made me cry. (make + 목적어 + 동사원형, to 안 씀)
- (X) I make a mistake yesterday. → (O) I made a mistake yesterday. (과거형)
- (X) It's made by wood. → (O) It's made of wood. (재질이 보이면 of, 변형되면 from)

## 연습

1. This song always _____ me feel better. (makes / does)
2. She _____ a big mistake at work. (made / did)
3. The team is _____ up of 10 people. (made / taken)
4. Does this _____ sense to you? (make / have)
5. We _____ it to the airport with 5 minutes to spare. (made / got)

**정답**: 1. makes  2. made  3. made  4. make  5. made
