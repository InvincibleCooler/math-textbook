# toward(s)

> '~쪽으로' — to보다 부드러운 방향, 도달이 아닌 지향

## 감각
### 방향
- She walked toward the door.
- The car is heading toward us.

### 태도
- What's your attitude toward this?

### 목적
- We're working toward a solution.
- Save toward your retirement.

## vs to
- **toward**: 방향만 (도착 X) — walking toward the station
- **to**: 도착 포함 — walking to the station

## 연습
1. She has a positive attitude _____ life. (toward / for)
2. He walked slowly _____ the exit. (toward / to) — 둘 다 가능

**정답**: 1. toward  2. toward/to
