# along

> 길을 따라 '나란히' — 동반과 진행

## 감각 확장
### 공간: ~을 따라
- Walk along the river.
- There are trees along the road.

### 동반: 함께
- Come along! We're leaving.
- Bring your friends along.

### 진행: 나아감
- The project is moving along nicely.
- How are you getting along?

## 동사 조합 TOP
| 동사 + along | 감각 | 예문 |
|get along|잘 지내다|Do you get along with your coworkers?|
|come along|함께 오다 / 진행되다|Things are coming along well.|
|go along with|동의하다|I'll go along with your plan.|
|move along|이동하다|Move along, nothing to see here.|

## 함정
- (X) I get along him. → (O) I get along with him. (with 필요)

## 연습
1. She gets _____ well with everyone. (along / on)
2. The work is coming _____ nicely. (along / up)
3. Walk _____ the path until you see the sign. (along / through)

**정답**: 1. along  2. along  3. along
