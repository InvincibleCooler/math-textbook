# within

> 경계를 넘지 않는 '안쪽'

## 감각
### 시간: ~이내
- I'll be there within an hour.
- Please reply within 3 days.

### 범위: 안쪽
- The target is within reach.
- Keep it within budget.

### 내면
- The answer lies within yourself.

## vs in
- **within**: 경계를 강조 (넘지 않는다) — within 24 hours
- **in**: 단순 안 — in 24 hours (24시간 후)

## 연습
1. We must finish _____ the deadline. (within / until)
2. The hotel is _____ walking distance. (within / in)

**정답**: 1. within  2. within
