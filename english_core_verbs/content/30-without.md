# without

> '함께 있지 않은' — with의 부정

## 감각
### 없이
- I can't live without coffee.
- She left without saying goodbye.

### ~하지 않고
- He solved it without any help.
- Without thinking, she jumped in.

## 핵심 패턴
- without + 명사: without money, without doubt
- without + -ing: without knowing, without telling me
- go without: I can't go without sleep.
- do without: We'll have to do without.

## 함정
- (X) without to say → (O) without saying (without + -ing)

## 연습
1. She passed the test _____ studying. (without / not)
2. I can't do _____ my morning coffee. (without / with)

**정답**: 1. without  2. without
