# as

> '같은 자격으로' — 역할, 비교, 이유, 동시

## 감각
### 역할: ~로서
- She works as a nurse.
- He acted as a translator.

### 비교: ~처럼
- As usual, he was late.
- Do as I say.

### 이유: ~이므로
- As it was raining, we stayed home.

### 동시: ~하면서
- As I walked in, everyone looked at me.

### 비교 구문
- as tall as, as soon as, as far as

## vs like
- **as**: 실제 역할/자격 — She works as a teacher. (선생님으로)
- **like**: 닮음/유사 — She acts like a teacher. (선생님처럼)

## 연습
1. She started her career _____ an intern. (as / like)
2. _____ I said before, we need more time. (As / Like)
3. He's not _____ tall _____ his brother. (as...as / so...like)

**정답**: 1. as  2. As  3. as...as
